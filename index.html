About Me

I am a software engineer who previously worked at a startup called Concourse Labs, that was later acquired by Fortinet. There I worked as a backend software engineer, mainly working on Java services but as a small company, worked in many places other than just Java code. I was able to gain experience in other languages and tools as well as some infrastructure pieces.

My developer interests lie as close to the data. I was fortunate enough to work solely on backend and not have to do any frontend work in my professional career so far as I much prefer working with and creating endpoints and services to fetch and process data. While I do have some education and internship experience dealing with frontend, it is not my area of expertise.

As a side project, I am also working with a group of friends in an indie game development studio called Kuneho Studios. There we are in the process of making our first title, Project Radiance - a multi-lane rhythm game with its own unique features, art, and music.

Other interests lie many in soccer and motorsport, both racing and photography (can see some here)
